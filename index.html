Help build the future of Highsec PvE!

Malevelon Roe is a Highsec PvE focused corporation specializing in:

  • Abyss
  • Triglavian Invasions
  • Pirate Forward Operating Bases
  • Mission Running
  • Moon mining
  • Combat Anomaly and Signature sites
  • Industry

While MALRO assets are focused on Highsec PvE combat, Members are encouraged to explore all aspects of EVE including Industry, Market, Mining, PVP and corporate support will be offered where possible.

As part of the FEDUP family Malevelon Roe prides itself on securing the federation against all enemies. Through our friends in Federation Uprising and Aideron Robotics, interested MALRO members gain access to:

  • Low/Null Sec PVP and PVE action
  • Painless membership transition to (and from) Gallente Faction Warfare and Sov Null entities.
